Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with a clean, moisturized face.
  2. Apply a lightweight, sheer foundation or BB cream to even out skin tone.
  3. Use a concealer only where needed to maintain a natural look.
  4. Set with a light dusting of translucent powder for a soft, fresh finish.
  5. Apply a subtle pink or peach blush to the apples of your cheeks.
  6. Groom and lightly fill in your eyebrows with a natural shade.
  7. Curl your lashes and apply a thin coat of mascara or opt for clear mascara.
  8. Use a tinted lip balm or sheer lipstick for a natural, healthy glow.
