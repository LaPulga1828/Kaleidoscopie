Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Apply a light eye shadow base to create an even surface.
  2. Use a light-colored shadow (beige or champagne) on the mobile eyelid.
  3. Apply a medium-colored shadow in the crease for depth.
  4. Line the upper lash line with a black or brown eyeliner pencil.
  5. Apply mascara to both upper and lower lashes for volume and length.
  6. Highlight the inner corner of the eyes for a fresh, youthful glow.
