Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with a clean and well-moisturized face.
  2. Apply a luminous primer to enhance skin radiance.
  3. Use a lightweight, dewy foundation to even out skin tone.
  4. Apply concealer only where needed to maintain a natural glow.
  5. Set with a light dusting of translucent powder for a soft finish.
  6. Add a subtle peach or pink blush for a fresh, youthful look.
  7. Apply highlighter to cheekbones, brow bones, and the bridge of the nose.
  8. Use neutral eyeshadows and a thin coat of mascara for a soft look.
  9. Finish with a tinted lip balm or sheer lipstick for a natural shine.
