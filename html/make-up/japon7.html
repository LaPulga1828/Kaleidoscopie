Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.
  2. Apply a high-coverage foundation for a flawless base.
  3. Use a light concealer under the eyes to brighten the area.
  4. Set the face with a translucent powder for a matte finish.
  5. Apply dark and bold eyeshadow shades, focusing on the outer corners.
  6. Use a thick black eyeliner to create an exaggerated winged effect.
  7. Apply false eyelashes on both the upper and lower lash lines for a doll-like appearance.
  8. Highlight the inner corners of the eyes and the brow bone.
  9. Finish with a nude or pink lipstick for a balanced look.
