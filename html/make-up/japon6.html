Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with a clean and primed eyelid.
  2. Apply a white or pastel eyeshadow base for vibrant color payoff.
  3. Use bold and bright colors like pink, blue, or yellow on the eyelid.
  4. Blend the colors creatively, adding gradients or unique shapes.
  5. Apply glitter or shimmer to the center of the lid for extra pop.
  6. Draw bold eyeliner, experimenting with dramatic wings or artistic lines.
  7. Use colorful or patterned false lashes for an eccentric effect.
  8. Add small stickers, gems, or decals for extra Harajuku flair.
  9. Finish with a light touch of mascara to blend natural and false lashes.
