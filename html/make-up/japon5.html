Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.
  2. Apply a light, dewy foundation for a porcelain finish.
  3. Use a brightening concealer under the eyes and on any imperfections.
  4. Set with a translucent powder for a soft and smooth effect.
  5. Apply a soft pink or peach blush on the apples of the cheeks.
  6. Define your eyebrows with a soft, natural shape.
  7. Use pastel or shimmery eyeshadows for a sweet, doll-like look.
  8. Apply eyeliner with a small wing to enhance eye shape.
  9. Use long, curled false lashes or multiple coats of mascara.
  10. Apply a glossy pink or red lipstick for a youthful touch.
